Lasagna

Description

This is claimed to be the best lasagna recipe in the world. How to make it?

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Onion and garlic
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
