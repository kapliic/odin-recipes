Egg Casserole

Description

This sausage, egg, and cheese biscuit casserole is a quick and easy breakfast for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Pork sausage
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Chop ingredients
  3. Combine ingredients
  4. Bake
  5. Let it rest
